OUR STORY

Built by Athletes,
For Athletes

FitForge was born in 2019 from a simple belief — everyone deserves access to world-class fitness coaching, nutrition science, and community. No gym required.

OUR MISSION

Democratize
World-Class Fitness

We believe the gap between knowing what to do and actually doing it is the biggest obstacle in fitness. FitForge closes that gap with AI-powered personalization, expert human coaching, and a community that holds you accountable.

Founded in Mumbai in 2019, we've grown from a scrappy 5-person startup to a global team of 120+ coaches, nutritionists, engineers, and designers — all united by a single obsession: making fitness transformation accessible to every person on Earth.

OUR VALUES

What We
Stand For

🔬

Evidence Over Hype

We reject fitness fads and shortcuts. Every feature, plan, and recommendation is grounded in peer-reviewed science and validated by certified experts.

🤝

Community First

Fitness is better together. Our challenges, leaderboards, and live events turn individual journeys into shared victories that motivate the whole tribe.

♾️

Sustainable Progress

We build habits, not crash diets. Our programs are designed for the long game — 5 years from now, not just 5 weeks.

🌍

Radical Accessibility

World-class coaching shouldn't cost ₹50,000/month. We make elite-level guidance available to everyone with a smartphone.

🔒

Privacy & Trust

Your health data is yours. We never sell it, never share it, and follow the strictest data security standards globally.

🚀

Relentless Innovation

We ship features weekly. Our team is obsessed with finding new ways technology can make fitness smarter, faster, and more personalized.
