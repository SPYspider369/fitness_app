EXPERT TIPS

Nutrition Rules
That Actually Work

01

Prioritize Protein First

Aim for 0.8–1g of protein per pound of bodyweight. High protein intake supports muscle synthesis, reduces hunger, and boosts metabolism.

02

Time Your Carbs Strategically

Eat the majority of your carbohydrates around your workouts — before for fuel, after for recovery. Reduce carbs on rest days.

03

Hydration is Non-Negotiable

Drink 35ml of water per kg of bodyweight daily. Add 500ml for every hour of training. Even mild dehydration drops performance by 10%.

04

Don't Fear Dietary Fat

Healthy fats from avocado, nuts, olive oil, and fatty fish support hormone production, brain health, and joint recovery. Aim for 25–35% of calories.
